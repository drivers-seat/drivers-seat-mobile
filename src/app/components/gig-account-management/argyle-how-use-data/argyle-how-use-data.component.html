How We Use Argyle
Driver’s Seat uses a service called Argyle to connect to rideshare and delivery platforms and automatically import trips and earnings into Driver’s Seat.
Specifically, Argyle gives us access to your past and future trip and profile data from the platforms linked by you.
Driver’s Seat only uses and stores the following data:
  • Your profiles
  • Your activities
  • Your vehicles
This data will be imported into the Driver’s Seat app to automate the trips and earnings functionality associated with your account so that you are no longer required to manually track your trips and earnings.
Driver’s Seat will not have access to any pay allocations data, which includes sensitive banking information.
